Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 17, 2026

Korean Random Defense ("the app") is an Android 2D random defense game based on historical Korean fortress defense battles. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app processes and how that information is used.

1. Personal Information Collected Directly by the App

The current version of the app does not collect or sell personal information through a self-operated server that directly identifies a user, such as name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, camera data, microphone data, or precise location information. The current version of the app does not provide a self-operated server, account registration, sign-in, chat, community, or cloud save feature.

The current build does not include the Google Mobile Ads SDK, Firebase, Google Play Games Services, a separate analytics SDK, in-app purchases, or subscription SDKs. Therefore, the current version of the app does not collect or transmit advertising ID, account information, payment information, or leaderboard scores by itself.

2. Game Data Processed on the Device

To run gameplay, the app may process game state in memory during app execution, such as the current stage, wave, unit placement, enemy progress, remaining lives, food, gunpowder, merit, and reward use status. The current version of the app does not transmit this gameplay state to a self-operated server.

If future versions add features such as resume, settings storage, stage unlocks, best records, or achievement progress, such data may be stored locally on the user's device to provide app features. If these features are actually added, this Privacy Policy and the Google Play Data safety declaration will be updated accordingly.

3. Advertising and Third-Party SDKs

The current public/test build does not include a real advertising SDK, and the development reward flow works only inside the app without real ads.

If the app includes Google AdMob or another advertising SDK in the future, the app may display a top banner ad or a rewarded ad only when the user clearly chooses to view it. In that case, Google or its advertising partners may process information such as advertising ID, device information, app information, IP address, approximate location, ad impressions, and ad interaction information for ad serving, ads personalization, frequency capping, fraud prevention, ad measurement, and service improvement.

If an advertising SDK is actually introduced, personalized or non-personalized ads may be shown depending on the user's device settings, Google account settings, consent status, and applicable laws. For more details about Google's advertising data practices, please see Google Advertising Privacy & Terms.

4. Android Permissions

The current version of the app does not request sensitive permissions such as camera, microphone, contacts, photos, or precise location. Depending on app features or future advertising SDK integration, internet and network state permissions may be used.

If an advertising SDK is actually included in the future and advertising ID permission or related ad features are used, this will be reflected in the app description, this Privacy Policy, and the Google Play Data safety declaration.

5. Purpose of Use

Game state processed inside the current app is used to provide gameplay features, including battle progress, unit summon, movement, star upgrade, salvage, reward application, and clear/failure result display.

If local storage features are added in the future, such information will be used only to provide app features such as resume, settings persistence, stage unlocks, and record display. If an advertising SDK is actually added in the future, advertising-related information may be processed according to the SDK's purposes, such as ad serving, fraud prevention, and ad measurement.

6. Retention and Deletion

The current version of the app does not store user gameplay data on a self-operated server. Battle state held in memory during app execution may no longer be retained after the app or battle is closed.

If local storage features are added in the future, users can delete locally stored app data by clearing app data/storage in Android settings or by uninstalling the app. If an advertising SDK is actually introduced, retention and deletion of advertising-related information processed by Google or other third parties will be managed according to those services' privacy policies, ad settings, applicable laws, and the user's consent/choices.

7. Third-Party Sharing and International Processing

The current version of the app does not transmit or sell game records or settings to a self-operated server. Because the current build does not include a real advertising SDK, analytics SDK, or leaderboard SDK, no third-party sharing occurs through those SDKs.

If Google AdMob, Google Play Games Services, an analytics SDK, or another third-party SDK is actually added in the future, those service providers may process information needed to provide their services, and such information may be processed outside the user's country. If such a change occurs, this Privacy Policy will be updated.

8. Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information that directly identifies users, including children, such as name, contact information, or precise location information by itself. If advertising or account-based services are added in the future, Google Account, Google Play, and advertising service age settings, parental consent, visibility settings, ads personalization settings, and applicable laws may apply.

현재 버전의 앱은 이름, 이메일 주소, 전화번호, 연락처, 사진, 카메라, 마이크, 정밀 위치 정보와 같이 사용자를 직접 식별하기 위한 개인정보를 자체 서버로 수집하거나 판매하지 않습니다. 현재 버전의 앱은 자체 운영 서버, 회원가입, 로그인, 채팅, 커뮤니티, 클라우드 저장 기능을 제공하지 않습니다.

현재 빌드에는 Google Mobile Ads SDK, Firebase, Google Play Games Services, 별도 분석 SDK, 인앱 결제 또는 구독 SDK가 포함되어 있지 않습니다. 따라서 현재 버전의 앱은 광고 ID, 계정 정보, 결제 정보, 리더보드 점수 등을 자체적으로 수집하거나 외부 서버로 전송하지 않습니다.

2. 기기 안에서 처리되는 게임 데이터

앱은 전투 진행을 위해 현재 스테이지, 웨이브, 유닛 배치, 적 진행도, 남은 생명, 군량, 화약, 전공, 보상 사용 여부와 같은 게임 상태를 앱 실행 중 메모리에서 처리할 수 있습니다. 현재 버전에서는 이러한 게임 상태를 자체 서버로 전송하지 않습니다.

향후 버전에서 이어하기, 설정 저장, 관문 해금, 최고 기록, 업적 진행도와 같은 기능이 추가되는 경우 해당 데이터는 게임 기능 제공을 위해 사용자의 기기에 로컬로 저장될 수 있습니다. 이러한 기능이 실제로 추가되면 본 개인정보처리방침과 Google Play Data safety 선언을 함께 업데이트하겠습니다.

현재 공개/테스트 빌드는 실제 광고 SDK를 포함하지 않으며, 개발용 보상 흐름은 실제 광고 없이 앱 내부에서만 동작합니다.

향후 앱이 Google AdMob 또는 다른 광고 SDK를 실제로 포함하게 되는 경우, 앱은 상단 배너 광고 또는 사용자가 명확히 선택한 보상형 광고를 표시할 수 있습니다. 이 경우 Google 또는 광고 파트너는 광고 제공, 광고 맞춤설정, 빈도 제한, 부정 사용 방지, 광고 성과 측정 및 광고 서비스 개선을 위해 광고 ID, 기기 정보, 앱 정보, IP 주소, 대략적인 위치 정보, 광고 노출 및 상호작용 정보 등을 처리할 수 있습니다.

광고 SDK가 실제로 도입되는 경우, 사용자의 기기 설정, Google 계정 설정, 동의 상태 및 관련 법령에 따라 맞춤형 광고 또는 맞춤설정되지 않은 광고가 표시될 수 있습니다. Google 광고 데이터 처리에 대한 자세한 내용은 Google 광고 관련 개인정보 보호 및 약관을 확인해 주시기 바랍니다.

현재 버전의 앱은 카메라, 마이크, 연락처, 사진, 정밀 위치와 같은 민감 권한을 요청하지 않습니다. 앱 기능 또는 향후 광고 SDK 도입 여부에 따라 인터넷 및 네트워크 상태 확인 권한이 사용될 수 있습니다.

향후 광고 SDK가 실제로 포함되어 광고 ID 권한 또는 관련 광고 기능이 사용되는 경우, 앱 설명, 본 개인정보처리방침 및 Google Play Data safety 선언에 반영하겠습니다.
