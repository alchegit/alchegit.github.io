Privacy Policy

📌 English

Color Master does not collect any personal data from users.

The core game works offline; however, the optional global ranking feature communicates with a remote server (Firebase) to upload and display scores.

Outside of this ranking exchange, no user inputs, behavior, or history leave the device.

This app may display Google ads. Some ads can be personalized based on device activity.

For details, please see Google Privacy & Ads Policy.
